Item Guide

Skybox: Types of Items

Ghost Writing Book: Used to detect Ghost writing. An entity may write words to communicate and warn the user.

The EMF Reader: A small remote that detects EMF Readings. The presence of a ghost can vary from its strength of 1 - 5, with 5 being the highest.

UV Flashlight: Used to identify ghostly fingerprints and footprints.

Ghost Radio: A special piece of equipment that can ask the ghost questions to get pieces of information. Only some ghosts respond to the radio, which can be used as evidence.

Night Vision Cameras: This special night vision DSLR Camera is used to identify a presence of ghost orbs.

Thermometer: The thernometer is used to detect the temperature of a room. Usually, a ghost's presence chill the area, but freezing temperatures can be used as evidence.

Types of Ghosts

Skybox: Types of Ghosts

Notes: Their growls are extremely loud

Spirit: Ghost Radio, Ghost Orb, Fingerprints/Footprints
Notes: Loud footsteps that deafen ears

Revenant: EMF 5, Ghost Writing, Freezing Temperatures
Notes: Known to throw objects if agitated

Oni: EMF 5, Ghost Radio, Ghost Writing
Notes: Despises the light

Poltergeist: EMF 5, Fingerprints/Footprints, Ghost Writing
Notes: Very shy

Demon: Ghost Writing, Ghost Radio, Fingerprints/Footprints
Notes: Extremely agressive.

Phasmophobia

You're a ghost scout looking to find and confirm the haunted spirit.Your job is simple, find the right ghost, to inform the ghost hunting team so they can bring the right equipment for the job. You are assigned to a small street house on the end of a block. We also believe the Ghost's name to be Mary Brown. That's all we know about her. However, you have many items at your disposal, and a notepad on the top-left of the page incase you get lost. Find and gather as much evidence as you can and report back to HQ once you finish. Good luck!

Rules: This is a non-linear mystery/horror game that allows you to find and discover the ghost from exploration and finding the right evidence. For help, there will be 2 skyboxes showing the types of evidence to use, and of the types of ghosts available. There will also be a map showing where you are currently located. Have fun!
